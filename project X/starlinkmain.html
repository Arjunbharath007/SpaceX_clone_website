SEPTEMBER 29, 2023

STARLINK MISSION

On Friday, September 29 at 10:00 p.m. ET, Falcon 9 launched 22 Starlink
satellites to low- Earth orbit from Space Launch Complex 40 (SLC-40) at
Cape Canaveral Space Force Station in Florida.

This was the 10th flight for the first stage booster supporting this mission,
which previously launched CRS-24, Eutelsat HOTBIRD 13F, OneWeb 1,
SES-18 and SES-19, and now six Starlink missions.
